# Privacy Policy — NIOS Unofficial

Last updated: 10-02-26

## 1. Overview

NIOS Unofficial is a student-run, open community platform created **by students, for students**.  
We publish guides, explanations, resources, and study support related to NIOS exams and processes in simplified form.

We are **not an official government or NIOS website**. We do not claim any official authority. All content is educational and community-contributed.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Information you provide voluntarily

We may collect information when you:

- Submit notes, files, or resources
- Contact us through forms or email
- Contribute content
- Send feedback or corrections

This may include:

- Name or username
- Email address
- Submitted documents or messages

We only collect what is necessary for communication and contribution handling.

---

### b) Automatic technical data

Like most websites, we may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

- IP address
- Browser type
- Device type
- Pages visited
- Date and time of visit
- Referring links

This is used for basic analytics, security, and performance monitoring.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Maintain and improve the website
- Review and publish community contributions
- Respond to queries
- Prevent spam or abuse
- Analyze site performance and usage
- Comply with platform and legal requirements

We do **not** sell personal data.
